Wednesday

Safe travels from Alabama to Texas. Be sure to stop at the Travel Information Center when you cross the state line. It’s very cool!

Once you're all settled in, we can eat dinner at a nearby local restaurant or grab the food to go.

Thursday


Join us for breakfast at one of our favorite local restaurants. Afterward, we'll explore North Dallas, and make a couple of stops along the way.

Friday

It’s Christmas Eve!
By this time, I’m sure we’ve worn you out a bit, so we’ll take it easy on you today. Start your day off with breakfast at our place!

Afterward, take the time to relax, take a few naps, get in some last minute shopping, or do whatever you’d like to do. You can reach out to us for lunch or dinner recommendations.

Ladies (and gents) – I recommend relaxing your feet at a nearby nail spa. You’ll be in for a treat!

Saturday

Merry Christmas, everyone!
Open your gifts at our place, and enjoy a catered dinner at 1:30 PM. We’ll watch football games and holiday movies, and play games.

When the sun sets, come with us for a small outing – be sure to bring some comfortable shoes.

Sunday

Tired yet? We won't do a whole lot on this day. Outside of the events below, feel free to relax, rest, or do whatever you'd like to do.

AM — catch some fantastic views of downtown Dallas from my job. We’ll end the night with dinner at a fun restaurant.

PM — we’ll have a family dinner at one of our favorite restaurants. Don’t get too dressy; it’s going to get a little messy!

Monday

Safe travels from Texas back to Alabama.

These plans aren’t set in stone, but they’re a general overview of what we’d like to do during your visit.
